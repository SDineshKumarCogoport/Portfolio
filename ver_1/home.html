I'm DK & here's what I do

I'm an inquisitive person who spends most of my time helping people communicate their ideas visually. I create 3D visualizations of product concepts and work on Digital Fabrication to provide insights on assembly and prototyping in maker spaces.

I have interests aligned to computational design and interaction design. The works of Neri Oxman, Tinker Hatfield, Frank Gehry and Zaha Hadid fascinates me.
